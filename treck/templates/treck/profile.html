Company Profile

One visit to the exotic country of Nepal is never enough. “Namaste” a greeting so warm keeps calling you back. Venture into the wild at Royal Chitwan National Park, water raft and canoe on the numerous fine rivers and climb the many mountains to have an experience of a lifetime or simply enjoy the land of legends with its natural beauties and traditions

Discover Himalayan Treks with her group of friendly exploration experts will help you reach remote cultures, environments, wildlife and experiences beyond your dreams. We strive to provide you with quality food, services, and equipment consistent with our exploration goals and responsibility for the environment.
We know there will be some uncertainty, some risks and some events that just can’t be planned but we are your partners in finding the places, people and nature to tease your sense of adventure and intellectual curiosity. Choose one or more of our trips in this information pack and let’s work out your needs. You shall be assured of our quality and personalized service at Discover Himalayan Treks (P) Ltd. Adventure is the business we know best.

Background:

Discover Himalayan Treks (P) Ltd’s Managing Director and owner of the company, Mr. Prem K. Dangol has, since 1982, planned and organized tours and led Treks and expeditions in Nepal and Tibet. His 15 years experience has stood him in good stead and with his team of 10 staff has provided excellent professional service to their clients.

Mission:

Discover Himalayan Trek’s mission is to provide value and Professional, Quality service to our customers. It is a partnership of friendship and discoveries.

Quality Service:

Discover Himalayan Treks has a proven record of operating top quality adventure holidays based on first hand experience. We listen to our customers’ needs and satisfaction. We monitor every aspect of our service in our constant effort to improve in order to provide you with an unforgettable experiencing classic Nepal.

Realize your dreams:

Discover Himalayan Treks can specially design tours, treks and expeditions to realize your dreams and needs. Alternatively you can choose from the already proven and accepted tours, treks and expeditions we organize. We feel that you should be given the right information to make your choice for the right company. Our detailed trip notes and regional portfolios are supplemented by numerous free official publications. Call or write us for details.

Expertise:

Discover Himalayan Treks (p) Ltd. has very experienced leaders and guides who are selected for their competence, friendliness, honesty, and intelligence.
The guides are local residents who know the territory intimately, speak the local languages, and have undergone training in the country’s natural history.
The leaders and guides spends their working life in the mountains of the world, This combined with their professional approach, exceptional talent, responsibility, patience, attention, enthusiasm and experience, can assure you of an unforgettable experience that results in lasting cross-culture friendship and wonderful memories.
